Training Overview

SAMBO & GRAPPLING

A highly efficient combat sports program blending the effective takedowns of Judo and SAMBO with modern BJJ (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu) and Wrestling.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

  • SAMBO, Wrestling and Judo style throws and positional takedowns.
  • Ground control, pins, and submissions.
  • Practical Self Defence application.
  • Competition readiness for SAMBO, BJJ and Wrestling rulesets.

WHY HAMILTON COMBAT SPORTS

Because Martial Arts are for everyone, whether you want to get fit, learn self defence or be a champion. Our instruction is led by coaches with a successful track record in coaching at state and national competition.
