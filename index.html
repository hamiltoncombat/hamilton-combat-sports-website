common questions

Frequently asked

1. What are the uniform requirements for class?
  • Grappling: Rash guard or fitted t‑shirt and shorts (no zippers or pockets).
  • SAMBO & Combat SAMBO: SAMBO uniform required (kurtka, shorts, wrestling shoes) – message coach for online store suggestions.
  • All gear must be clean and nails trimmed for hygiene and safety.
2. Do I need to be fit to start?
No. Everyone starts somewhere. Our classes are structured so you build fitness as you learn technique. Go at your own pace.
3. How old do I need to be?
Our classes are for adults and teens aged 14 and over. Minors need a parent or guardian to sign a waiver.
4. Will I get injured?
We prioritise safety. Warm‑ups, supervised drilling, and no sparring without coaching approval. Minor bruises can happen, but serious injury is rare.
5. Do you spar / roll on the first night?
No. Your first few sessions focus on basic movements, breakfalls, and positional drills. Sparring is optional and only after you're comfortable.
6. What if I have no grappling experience at all?
That's normal. Most of our members started as beginners. Our first class covers the absolute basics – no prior knowledge needed.
7. Can I just watch a class before joining?
Yes. You're welcome to observe a session. Just message ahead so we know you're coming.
8. What if I can't make it every week?
That's fine. Train when you can. There's no attendance requirement or contract lock‑in.
9. Do you have changing rooms or showers?
The venue has changing facilities and showers. Most students arrive ready to train.
10. Can I bring a friend to the free trial?
Yes. They can also book a free trial. Just let us know in advance so we have space.
